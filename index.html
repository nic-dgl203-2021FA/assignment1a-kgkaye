Featured Projects

Below is a sample of projects from my past year at school

Zeal Fitness App Advert

Class: Typography. The assignment was to make a one page advert employing the use of expressive typography. This advert is for Zeal Fitness, a fitness app that I designed for a UI/UX class (example below).

Call To Action Poster

Class: Intro To Graphic Design For the assignment we were to make a call to action poster on the topic of an issue of our choice. We were to borrow the style from another graphic designer. My poster covers the opioid crisis in the style of David Carson.

Zeal Fitness App.

Class: UI/UX From a topic provided for the assignment I designed a fictional app - Zeal Fitness. This is a personalized fitness app that tracks the user’s progress and provides them searchable workout videos.

Hydro lines - Short Video (still)

Hydro Lines is a 1 minute short video that cycled through various scenes of power lines with mild visual effects. The audio was created by myself.
